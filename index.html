How I got here

I started my journey into Computer Science my junior year of high school in 2017. I decided to take a Computer Science Elective Course and in this class my Passion and Admiration of Computer Science Sprouted and Blossomed. I found it not only fascinating but that writing and creating code came naturally. The projects I had in this class I'd finish rapidly leading me to add additional features and work on personal project ideas. This enviroment fostered my curiousity as I'd venture to other sources outside of the classroom to achieve these ideas. This class convinced me to pursue my Computer Science Degree. While pursuing my degree I was able to meet my peers, learning for some and teaching others. My knowledge of the many sub-disciplines grew and I was unsure which of the many possible paths before me to which I should commit. It was after conversing with experienced Software Engineers I was reminded of my initial adoration for the creating and writing of code; that lead me to pursue Software Engineering. I then finished my degree in January 2024. So far post-graduation I've been lucky enough to land a Fellowship at Headstarter AI, allowing me to further hone my skills and better prepare myself as a promising candidate for what opportunities await me.
